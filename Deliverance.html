Yair Etziony

Deliverance


You are my hiding place; You will protect me from trouble And surround me with songs of deliverance.

“Deliverance can happen anywhere and anytime, it can find you in a old dusty pub or just loom somewhere in an empty train station”. This is the main idea behind the new album from Berlin based composer Yair Etziony.

The 6th full album from Yair Etziony is his most physical work to date; all tracks in “Deliverance” have a specific dark tone and a unique physical quality to them. This is Etziony’s first full album since he moved from Tel Aviv to Berlin, and one can hear how this change of residency changed his sound design and compositions.

The whole album has a darker, brooding energy to it; one might say it could fit as soundtrack for a dark science fiction movie. Using a wide array of instruments and tools from modular synths, vintage Roland gear and state of the line software, Etziony created a unique pallet of sound, connecting ideas from the 70s 80s to now days.

Release date: 10 March 2018 via Words & Sound Digital
Artwork by Tuvia Kudashevich
Mastered by Manes at Salz-Mastering
