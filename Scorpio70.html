Scorpio70

Scorpio70

Did you ever enjoyed watching a grown man dressed as a monster and destroying a toy city? Did you ever watch German porn movies from a worn-out VHS cassette? Did you watch too many sci-fi movies with scenes of people dressed in weird uniforms in large rooms full of dials and revolving tapes? Are you sure that space is the final frontier?

If you answered YES to all the questions above then we have just the right soundtrack for you: pounding drums coated with repetitive bass lines & holy-analogue-synth-goodness, taken straight from an old foreign weird B-movie - this is what it might sound like if Fassbinder was to direct a sci-fi B-flick with Neu! designated on the soundtrack.

Scorpio 70 is the result of locking up 3 imaginative musicians in a small recording studio with a bunch of instruments & analog toys without rehearsals or planning - a pure musical expression.

Scorpio70:
Guy Bibi (Proyect Hagumiya\We are Ghosts) - Drums and electronic percussions.
Benjamin Esterlis (Morphlexis\We are Ghosts) - Bass, synths and vocals.
Yair Etziony (Farthest South\False Industries) - Roland Juno6\Roland SH101\Korg MS20 Mini.

Release on 6-June-2014

Artwork is done by Tuvia Kudashevitz & Morphlexis

Mixed by Morphlexis, Mastering by Manes@Salz-Mastering
